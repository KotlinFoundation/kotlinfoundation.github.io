title: "kmp Contest Starter Kit 2026 Grant Recipient"
date: 2026-05-31 14:30
---

The Kotlin Foundation is pleased to announce the recipient of the KMP Contest Starter Kit Grant: Mirzamehdi Karimov.

The Kotlin Foundation team responsible for evaluating the submissions was genuinely impressed by the thoughtfulness of the submissions and shares a heartfelt thank you to everybody who took the time to share their ideas of what a compelling KMP Contest Starter Kit would look like.

As far as next steps, the Kotlin Foundation will work with the grant recipient over the course of the next 2+ months to get the starter kit repo and associated content published. It will be available for participants of the 2027 KMP Contest.

Read more about the grant: [Create This Year’s KMP Contest Starter Kit](/news/kmp-contest-starter-kit-2026/).
